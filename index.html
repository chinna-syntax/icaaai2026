SRKREC

Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKR), established in 1980, is one of Andhra Pradesh's first self-financing engineering institutions, dedicated to empowering rural students through technical education. With its 30-acre green campus, the college offers state-of-the-art facilities that foster an inclusive and culturally responsive learning environment. Over the years, SRKR has built a strong ethos of creativity, confidence, and logical thinking in its students, preparing them for nation-building and diverse careers.

The college's vision, inspired by its founder Sri Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju, continues under the leadership of Sri S. Prasad Raju, ensuring academic excellence. SRKR combines science, creativity, and critical thinking to address societal challenges, making it a hub for innovation and problem-solving. With 44 years of academic excellence, SRKR offers 12 B.Tech., 6 M.Tech., and 10 Ph.D. programs, alongside a B.B.A. program.

Accredited with an ‘A+’ Grade by NAAC and multiple NBA accredited B.Tech. programs, the institution is recognized by AICTE and UGC for its outstanding contributions to education and research. SRKR’s initiatives include the AICTE IDEA Lab emphasizing entrepreneurship, innovation, and skill development. Its 14 Centres of Excellence further drive research culture, solving societal challenges through research and development.

Department CSE

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College, established in 1991, has grown into a leading hub for teaching, research, and industry collaboration.The department offers B.Tech programmes in CSE, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Computer Science & Design, and CSE (IoT & Cyber Security including Blockchain Technology), along with M.Tech in Computer Science & Technology and a Ph.D. programme. It also pioneers a Minor Degree in Quantum Technologies, aligned with India’s National Quantum Mission.

With a team of highly qualified faculty, the department is recognized as a Research Centre under JNTU Kakinada. Graduates are consistently placed in top multinational companies and product-based firms across domains such as software development, data science, cybersecurity, AI/ML, and cloud computing. The department has also hosted three international conferences and actively promotes innovation through hackathons, design sprints, and professional society activities.

ICAAAI Conference

The 3rd International Conference on Algorithms in Advanced Artificial Intelligence (ICAAAI-2025) provides a global platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to exchange knowledge and showcase innovations in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and emerging computing technologies.

Building on the success of its previous editions, the conference emphasizes both theoretical advances and real-world applications across interdisciplinary domains.

Integrity & Standards

About Publication and Ethics Statement

The 3rd International Conference on Algorithms in Advanced Artificial Intelligence (ICAAAI-2026) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of publication ethics and research integrity. To ensure the quality and validity of all published proceedings, the conference organizers expect all authors, reviewers, and editors to strictly adhere to the guidelines set forth by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).

Double-Blind Peer Review: Every submitted manuscript undergoes a rigorous double-blind peer review process conducted by at least two independent domain experts. Reviewers evaluate manuscripts based on originality, technical quality, research methodologies, and contribution to the field of artificial intelligence. To ensure impartiality, all identifying author information is removed from the manuscripts prior to review.

Plagiarism & AI-Generated Content Policies: Originality is a prerequisite for submission. The conference enforces a strict plagiarism detection protocol using industry-standard tools (such as Turnitin/iThenticate). The similarity index of any submission must remain strictly below 10–12%. Furthermore, manuscripts containing excessive or uncredited machine-generated content are not considered for publication.

Authorship & Academic Misconduct: Only individuals who have made significant intellectual contributions to the research may be listed as authors. Any form of academic misconduct, including plagiarism, data fabrication, duplication, or redundant publication, will be handled with absolute seriousness. Violations will result in the immediate retraction of the paper and notification of the authors' home institutions.
